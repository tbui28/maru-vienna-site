About Us

Maru has been proudly serving the Vienna community since November 4th, 2016, from its cozy location in the Vienna Shopping Center. We bring together authentic Korean cuisine and fresh Japanese sushi under one roof. Our chefs blend traditional recipes with modern presentation, offering a unique fusion dining experience. Whether you're craving bulgogi, miso ramen, sushi rolls, or bento boxes, we prepare every dish with premium ingredients and care. We welcome families, couples, and friends to relax in our warm yet elegant space. We also offer convenient take-out and lunch specials. Our mission is to share a piece of Korean and Japanese culture, one plate at a time.
