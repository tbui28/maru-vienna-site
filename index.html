About Us

Maru has proudly served the Vienna community since November 4th, 2016, and we are truly grateful for all the love and support over the years. As we continue to grow, we’re excited to share that Maru Korean Cuisine & Sushi is now joining with our second location, Rateba, and moving to a new home in Fairfax. You can now find us at Maru Ramen & Korean Cuisine, located in the Pan Am Shopping Center, near Micro Center. We continue to serve the same authentic Korean dishes, fresh sushi, and delicious ramen you love — now all in one place. Thank you for being part of our journey. We warmly invite you to visit us at our new Fairfax location and enjoy a great meal with us again!
