About Us

Maru is a locally beloved restaurant in Vienna, VA, bringing authentic Korean cuisine and fresh Japanese sushi under one roof. Our chefs blend traditional recipes with modern presentation, offering a unique fusion dining experience. Whether you're craving bulgogi, miso ramen, sushi rolls, or bento boxes, we prepare every dish with premium ingredients and care. We welcome families, couples, and friends to relax in our cozy yet elegant space. We also offer convenient take-out and lunch specials. Our mission is to share a piece of Korean and Japanese culture, one plate at a time.
